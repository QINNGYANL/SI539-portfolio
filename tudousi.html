Fried Potato Slices

Step 1: Peel and shred potatoes, rinse and set aside.

Step 2: Slice garlic, cut green onion into sections, and shred ginger.

Step 3: Add oil to the pot and heat it up. Add a little lard to taste better. Add ginger, garlic and shallot.

Step 4: Add potato shreds and fry on high heat. After the potatoes change color, add salt, a little soy sauce, and then add a little spicy and fresh, add chicken essence and cook.
