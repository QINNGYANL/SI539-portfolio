Sweet and Sour Pork Ribs

Step 1: Blanching 500 grams of short ribs.

Step 2: Marinate with one tablespoon of cooking wine, one tablespoon of light soy sauce, half a tablespoon of dark soy sauce, and two tablespoons of balsamic vinegar (not white vinegar) for 20 minutes.

Step 3: Take out, wash and control water for later use, fry them to golden color.

Step 4: Put the ribs in the pot, the water for marinating the ribs, and three tablespoons of sugar.

Step 5: Simmer for ten minutes on low heat to collect juice.
