Grilled Lobster

Step 1: Peel and mince garlic, add butter and a pinch of salt and fry until golden.

Step 2: Wash the lobsters and cut them in half, neatly arrange them on the baking tray, spray a little cooking wine, and spread the fried garlic on top.

Step 3: Preheat the oven to 180 degrees, put the lobster into the oven, 180 degrees for 12 minutes.

Step 4: Take it out and grind some Parmesan cheese on it. Return to the oven and heat/grill for 3 to 5 minutes until the surface is crispy and golden.

Step 5: Bon Appétit！
